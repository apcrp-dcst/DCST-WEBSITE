About Our Department

Founded in 1950, the department of computer science and technology has been at the forefront of technological innovation and education for nearly four decades. Our mission is to provide students with the knowledge and skills needed to tackle the computing challenges of tomorrow.

With over 5 faculty members and 180 students, we offer a comprehensive range of undergraduate programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in industry, research, and academia.

Our research areas include artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, computer graphics, algorithms.
